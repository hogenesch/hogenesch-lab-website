Join the Lab

The lab welcomes students, postdoctoral fellows, and collaborators interested in circadian biology, systems genomics, computational methods, and circadian medicine.

Who Fits Well

  • Experimental biologists interested in clock mechanisms, physiology, or disease.
  • Computational scientists working on transcriptomics, statistical methods, or rhythmic data analysis.
  • Clinician-scientists interested in human time-of-day biology, chronotherapy, or translational circadian medicine.
  • Collaborators who value open resources, clear quantitative reasoning, and reproducible analysis.

What to Send

  • A short note describing scientific interests and fit with the lab.
  • A current CV.
  • Links to papers, preprints, code, datasets, or other representative work when relevant.
