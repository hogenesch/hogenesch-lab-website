Practical Use

  • Use JTK_CYCLE or MetaCycle for rhythmicity detection in structured time-series experiments.
  • Use PSEA for questions about pathway timing rather than isolated genes.
  • Use CYCLOPS when samples lack collection times but still preserve circadian structure.
  • Use CircaDB and the circadian atlas to move from a gene or tissue to a published rhythmic-expression context.
