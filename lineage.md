  <h1>Scientific Lineage</h1>
  <p class="lede">
    The Hogenesch Lab builds on intellectual traditions in chemical biology,
    circadian biology, and functional genomics.
  </p>
</header>

<section class="theme-block">
<p>The scientific direction of the lab was shaped by training with Christopher A. Bradfield, Joseph Takahashi, Peter G. Schultz, and Steve A. Kay. Their work helped define the experimental rigor, conceptual range, and interdisciplinary style that continue to inform the lab's approach to biological timing and genome-scale biology.</p>
