Scientific Lineage

The Hogenesch Lab builds on intellectual traditions in core molecular clock work, genomics, systems biology, and circadian medicine. The scientific direction of the lab was shaped by training with several exceptional mentors: Christopher A. Bradfield, Joseph S. Takahashi, Peter G. Schultz, and Steve A. Kay.
